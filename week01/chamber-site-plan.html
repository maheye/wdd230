Site Name: Hsinchu Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Hsinchu Chamber of Commerce provides information services to semiconductor companies to promote semiconductor cooperation and achieve win-win results.

Scenarios

As Hsinchu's population grows at an annual rate of 2.72%, coupled with Taiwan's global semiconductor status, more and more companies want to cooperate with each other to seek win-win results. We will promote and help Taiwan's semiconductor companies cooperate with each other by holding small semiconductor conferences. Help companies share their products and services. We will provide company introductions and contact information on the website, and share information through offline gatherings.
